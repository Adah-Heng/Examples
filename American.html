Mac and Cheese

Recipe

  • 3 Tablespoons of white flour.
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter.
  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 2 Cups of sharp cheader cheese

Directions

  1. Parically fill a saucepan with water with a pinch of salt and bring to a boil.
  2. Melt the butter in the other saucepan.
  3. Add flour and mixed till combined.
  4. Add the noodles to the boiling water.
  5. Add the milk in a little bit making sure it is thickened before adding more until milk is gone.
  6. Add the cheese.
  7. Combine the cheese sauce with noodles.

Please note that you can add any type of milk you want, but I recommond 2% or higher and feel free to make more if needed.
You can also add more milk or flour depending on if the cheese sauce needs thickening or thinning. A saucepan is
picutred above.
