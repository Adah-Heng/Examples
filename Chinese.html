Spicy pepper beef

Recipe

  • Steak or a beef roast
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • Bell peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Red pepper flakes
  • sesame seed oil
  • Ginger
  • Rice

Directions

  1. Make the rice by adding same ratio of rice to water
  2. Cut the beef into bitesized chuncks or strips.
  3. Use enough soy sauce to cover the beef and add the ground black pepper.
  4. Add beef to soy sauce mixture.
  5. Marinade the beef. If you use steak only for 3 to 5 hours. If you use roast marinade for the day before and until time to cook
  6. Cut up the bell peppers into bitesized chuncks.
  7. Add a little bit of oil to the hot wok and wait till the oil heats up.
  8. Add the ginger and stir till fragrant.
  9. Add the red pepper flakes and stir till fragrant.
  10. Add the beef and the marinade and stir the beef until cooked.
  11. Remove the beef into a sperate bowl.
  12. Add a bit more oil and place the peppers in.
  13. Cook the peppers until a bit soft on the out side.
  14. Add the beef back in
  15. Serve with the rice

Please note that you can make your own quanities and the spice level. I suggest you make this recipe in a wok a traditional chinese pan
which is picuted above. For the rice you can use an instant pot, rice cooker, or sauce pan.
