Chicken tikka masala

Recipe

  • Greek yogurt
  • Curry powder
  • 2 Tablespoons of oil
  • onions
  • Raw boneless chicken
  • Heavy wipping cream
  • Tomato puree

Directions

  1. Cut the chicken into bitesized pieces.
  2. Mix half of the curry powder in with the greek yogurt.
  3. Marinade over night till time to cook.
  4. Cook the chicken in a pan with some oil untill fully cooked and a crisp outside.
  5. Put the oil into the cast iron pan or dutch oven.
  6. Put the onions and carmalize the onions.
  7. Add the rest of the curry powder into the pan stir until fregrant.
  8. Add the tomato puree.
  9. Add the chicken into the mix.
  10. Add the heavy wipping cream.

Please note that you can add as much or as little as you like. You can use either cast iron
skillet or dutch oven depending on the amount of food, which are pictured above.
I suggest you use a regular 3.5oz containor to split in half or use two containors depending
on the spice level you want. That means that one containor will go into the chicken
marinade, and the other into the pan. Make sure that you add enough onion meaning a little
bit of sauce then a little bit of onoin.
I suggest a ratio of 1 can of 16oz of tomato
puree to one small onoin.
