Sushi

Recipe

  • Some type of protein such as pork, cooked shrimp, beef, chicken, cold smoked salom.
  • Link for Sushi rice recipe
  • Some vetables such as cucumbers, green onoins, avacado, bell peppers
  • Cream cheese
  • Nori paper aslo as seaweed paper for sushi

Directions

  1. If you choose to marinade some types of meat then I would go find a recipe that would fit the type of meat
  2. Cook the meat with the exption of smoked salmon
  3. Make the sushi rice recipe as directed.
  4. Cut up your choice of vetables into thin slices.
  5. You can either use a whole or half of a sheet depending on how thick you want the roll
  6. Place the nori paper on the mat
  7. Place a bit of the sushi rice on the nori and spread until coated if you need more you can put more on.
  8. Place your vegtables and meat near the end near you making sure that there is a bit of room for the role to have a spot join on the other side.
  9. Roll the sushi.
  10. To cut the sushi use a sharp knife dipped in water. You might have to re-dip the knife to keep it from sticking

Please note that you can use a mold to make differnt shapes. The sushi roll mat is also called makisu.
