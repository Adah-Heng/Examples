Green chile enchiladas with beans and rice

Recipe

  • 7 to 8 inch flour tortillas.
  • 1 pound of our choice of ground beef or chicken.
  • large onion
  • 1 block of cream cheese
  • 4 Tablespoons of Chile powder
  • 4 Tablespoons of Chile powder, paprika
  • 2 Tablespoons of onoin, garlic powder
  • 1 can of 28oz Old El Paso Mild Green Chile Enchilada Sauce
  • 1 block of queso
  • Link for refried beans
  • Link for mexican rice

Directions

  1. Add the beef or chicken and onion and cook till the beef or chicken is fully cooked in a frying pan.
  2. Add the spices in.
  3. Add the cream cheese and stir till melted.
  4. Add some of the beef or chicken mixture to one tortilla and roll into a roll.
  5. Place the tube into a greased casserole dish.
  6. Keep doing this until pan is ful.
  7. Open the can and dump the sauce onto the enchiladas making sure spread evenly a possible.
  8. Add as much the shreaded queso as you like.
  9. Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.

Please note that you can add more or less depending on how much you need.
